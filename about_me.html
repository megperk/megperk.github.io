About Me

Registered dietitian with over a decade of professional work focused on transitioning into the field of data analytics and management. I am highly motivated to leverage my skills and knowledge to move into a role supporting and developing data-driven decisions and processes.

Professional with over 10 years of experience in the field of nutrition, I am excited about the prospect of applying my well-rounded background to a data-driven role that aligns with my strong interests and recent training in data analytics. My career in nutrition has instilled in me a passion for data-driven decisions, data visualization, and meticulous organization. I have actively pursued coursework in MS Excel, SQL, and Tableau to sharpen my skills, and I am eager to leverage these tools to extract valuable insights from data. My proficiency in Excel and Smartsheets, as well as my commitment to continuous improvement in these data tools, are evident in my daily work. Throughout my career, I have consistently excelled in proactively identifying opportunities to enhance efficiency. Whether advocating for programmatic changes to align with strategic objectives or designing and documenting procedures to optimize resource utilization, I am driven by a desire to streamline processes and produce tangible results. My experience, skills, keen attention to detail, and proven track record as a quick and adaptable learner position me as a strong candidate for the a data-focused role. I am enthusiastic about applying my expertise to contribute to the strategic utilization of data.
